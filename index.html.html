Our story

A living room for the neighbourhood

café culture opened as a simple idea: coffee brings people together, and a good room lets them stay. Regulars come for the quiet — students with headphones and notebooks, writers nursing a single espresso for an afternoon, old friends catching up over mint tea.

It's earned a loyal following exactly for that reason — locals describe it as one of the most peaceful spots in Casablanca to work, read or just enjoy a slow breakfast.
